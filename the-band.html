Who Are We?

Background

A 6-piece wedding band that formed in Newcastle in 2010, we play a range of different styles including: pop, funk, rock and disco. We're a high energy group who live to perform and love to get people up and dancing. We've been performing long enough to know that every wedding needs something a little different, and we've got the experience to make sure the dance floor is full all night!
